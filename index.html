Citizen Labs

Developers, designers, data geeks, leaders, and idea-makers who volunteer to help Grand Rapids' government and civic organizations increase civic engagement by adopting open web technologies.

Come To Our Meetings

View our Meetup site or check out our Facebook page for the next location and time. We meet the first and third Tuesday of every month. Some meetings are focused on a specific project, at others we organize our efforts and brainstorm new projects. Our meetups are casual and anyone is welcome to join. Food and drink are often involved.
